	<p>We offer a bundle of CES courses that include a field work component. This bundle is called the ACRE Supplement. ACRE is a national membership organization that reviews and approves curriculum. This ACRE Supplement bundle includes 11 ACRE-approved courses (Part 1) plus activities and field work (Part 2).</p>

<h2>How can my staff earn an ACRE Certificate?</h2>
<p>To earn an ACRE Certificate, learners must complete the 2-part ACRE Supplement training series:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Part 1:</strong> Pass the final quiz on each of the 11 courses with a score of 80% or higher</li>
  <li><strong>Part 2:</strong> Participate in 8 hours of supplemental online learning activities, which involves fieldwork in your community. Watch three 30-minute–1-hour webinars supplementing their CES coursework</li>
</ul>
<p>After completing this 2-part training series, learners can earn the nationally recognized ACRE certificate. Their names will be added to the ACRE Certificate Registry.</p>

<ul>

  <li>CRC-credit eligible</li>
</ul>
